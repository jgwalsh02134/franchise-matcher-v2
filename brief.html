AdSell.ai Local Presence Pilot  ·  90-Day Multi-Market Flight

Your brand, inside the local publications your customers already trust.

The AdSell.ai Local Presence Pilot puts your brand in trusted local and community publications across your operating markets — print and digital — with one order, one payment, and a response report at the end.
Buying local media across many markets has meant separate negotiations with every publication. The Local Presence Pilot replaces that with one campaign request through AdSell.ai, the marketplace connecting advertiser demand with publisher inventory — powered by Vision Data, the software local and community publishers have relied on since 1973.
HOW IT WORKS
1
Match. We select trusted local and community publications aligned to your markets — the pilot books one publication in each of eight markets.
2
Approve. You approve the plan. Each publication reviews and approves your ad before it runs — your brand appears with the publication's consent, inside the pages their readers trust.
3
Run. A 90-day flight, managed end to end by the AdSell.ai team.
4
Count. Promo codes, QR codes, and tracked numbers on every placement. You get a response report by market and publication — counted responses, plainly reported.
Presence, not proximity. Most digital advertising places your brand near a community on the open web. The Local Presence Pilot places it inside the publications that community already reads and trusts — with every placement reviewed and approved by the publication itself.
